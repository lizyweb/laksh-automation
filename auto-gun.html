Automatic Pump Controller (AUTO GUN)

Introducing AUTO GUN, an Automatic Pump Controller that sets a new standard in pump system management. Engineered with precision and reliability in mind, AUTO GUN is equipped with cutting-edge features that ensure optimal pump performance in a variety of operational scenarios.

One of the key highlights of AUTO GUN is its real-time monitoring capabilities, providing indicators for Power On, Motor On, Sump Empty, Tank Full, Low Voltage, Dry Run, Overload, Single Phase Protection, and Reverse Phase.
